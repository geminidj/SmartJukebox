It appears jukebox playback is paused

It could be because it is after 0030, and the jukebox is disabled until 1030.

It may also be because someone is playing a game on the projector, and the playback is paused so they can hear their game.

Don't worry, you can still make requests, and vote on songs.

To get rid of this warning, wait until the jukebox playback has resumed and refresh the page, or request another song.

If you frequently see this warning when the Jukebox is working normally, update your system time and refresh the page.
